## 🔄 Converting to PNG (If Needed)

While SVG is recommended, you can convert to PNG:

**Online Tools (Free):**
- CloudConvert.com
- SVG2PNG.com
- Convertio.co

**Recommended PNG Sizes:**
- Favicon: 32×32, 64×64 px
- Social Media: 512×512, 1024×1024 px
- Website: 256×256, 400×400 px
- Print: 2048×2048, 4096×4096 px
